About Us

Welcome to Brain-Link, an app that transforms the way we collaborate as students. "Brain-Link has transformed the way we collaborate as students. It's more than just an app; it's a platform that fosters a sense of community and shared learning. The ability to connect with peers, share knowledge, and work together on projects is a game-changer for ones academic journey.

This project is part of our portfolio at Holberton School, showcasing our skills and passion for software engineering. It is a representation of our dedication to learning and innovation.

Key Features

Create Group Chat

The students are able to create chats and discuss on various projects and also socialize

Real time messaging

Allows users to send and receive messages instantly

User Authentication

This functionality allows users to create a new account if they don't have one or log in using existing credentials if they already have an account.
